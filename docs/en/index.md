## What makes this a handbook rather than a software manual?

The handbook starts with humanities questions. Tools enter only after the object of study, evidence, data model and interpretive claim have been made explicit. Every substantial method is paired with validation, uncertainty, rights and reproducibility. The printed-page metaphor is not the governing form: stable releases can be reviewed and archived, while the living edition can correct broken instructions and grow through credited contributions.

!!! info "Publication status"
    This repository is a transformed and expanded successor to the *Digital Linguistics Playbook*. Version `0.1.0` establishes the bilingual architecture and publication model. A future `v1.0` will be the first formally reviewed edition.
